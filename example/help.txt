Usage:
    add <new todo>
    remove <num/*> -- index of row you want to delete or * for all rows
    list [match] -- list all or `match`ing TODOs
    see_diff -- show your current time diff settings
    set_diff -- adds your own time diff (how long it takes to send you another warning) (in seconds)
    poke <username> -- manually remember user that he has some TODO
